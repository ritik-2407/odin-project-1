Chole Bhature Recipe

Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) and deep-fried bread (bhature). Begin by cooking chickpeas with onion-tomato masala and spices until rich and flavorful. For the bhature, prepare a soft dough using all-purpose flour, yogurt, and a leavening agent, then let it rest. Roll out and deep fry the dough into puffed golden bhature. Serve hot with onion salad, pickle, and lemon wedges for a delicious traditional meal.

Ingredients

  • Dried chickpeas (chole)
  • Onions and tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (coriander, cumin, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric)
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Salt and oil

Steps :

  1. Soak and Boil Chickpeas: Soak 1 cup dried chickpeas overnight. Pressure cook with salt until soft.

  2. Prepare Chole: Sauté chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomatoes. Add spices and cooked chickpeas. Simmer until gravy thickens.

  3. Make Bhature Dough: Mix 2 cups all-purpose flour with ½ cup yogurt, ¼ tsp baking powder, a pinch of baking soda, salt, and a little water. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 2–3 hours.

  4. Fry Bhature: Divide dough into balls, roll them out, and deep-fry in hot oil until puffed and golden.

  5. Serve: Serve hot bhature with spicy chole, onion salad, pickle, and lemon wedges.
