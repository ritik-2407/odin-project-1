Pizza Recipe

Description

To make a classic pizza, start by preparing a soft dough using flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil, then let it rise. Roll it out into a thin base, spread tomato sauce evenly, and top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings like vegetables, pepperoni, or olives. Bake it in a preheated oven at around 220°C (430°F) for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Finish with a sprinkle of herbs like oregano or basil before serving hot.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Active dry yeast
  • Water
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • Toppings

Steps :

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large bowl, mix 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp dry yeast. Add ¾ cup lukewarm water and 1 tbsp olive oil. Knead into a soft, smooth dough. Cover and let it rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size.

  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F) for at least 10–15 minutes.

  3. Shape the Base: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a round or rectangular shape on a floured surface. Transfer to a greased or parchment-lined baking tray.

  4. Add Sauce and Toppings: Spread a thin layer of tomato/pizza sauce over the base. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly, then add your desired toppings (like veggies, pepperoni, etc.).

  5. Bake the Pizza: Bake in the preheated oven for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove from the oven, sprinkle with oregano or fresh basil, slice, and serve hot.
