Egg Curry Recipe

Description

Egg curry is a flavorful Indian dish made by simmering boiled eggs in a spicy onion-tomato gravy. The curry is enriched with ginger, garlic, and aromatic spices, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. Best served hot with rice or roti, egg curry is a quick and easy option for a protein-packed lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Boiled eggs
  • Onions and tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps :

  1. Boil Eggs: Boil 4–6 eggs, peel them, and make slight slits to absorb the flavors.

  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil in a pan, sauté chopped onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.

  3. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Mix in spices and cook until oil separates.

  4. Add Eggs: Add the boiled eggs and coat them well in the masala. Pour some water to make a curry and simmer for 5–10 minutes.

  5. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
