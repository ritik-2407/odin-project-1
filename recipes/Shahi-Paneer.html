Shahi Paneer Recipe

Description

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a luscious gravy of tomatoes, onions, cashews, and aromatic spices. The dish is finished with cream for a royal touch, making it perfect for special occasions. Serve it hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice to enjoy its full flavor.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cubed)
  • Onions and tomatoes
  • Cashew nuts
  • Fresh cream
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder)
  • Butter or ghee
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Prepare Gravy Base: Sauté chopped onions, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and cashews in butter. Cook until soft, then blend into a smooth paste.

  2. Cook the Gravy: In a pan, heat butter or ghee, add the paste and spices. Cook until the mixture releases oil.

  3. Add Paneer: Add paneer cubes and a little water. Simmer for 5–7 minutes so the flavors absorb well.

  4. Finish with Cream: Stir in fresh cream and cook for 2 more minutes. Garnish with coriander or a drizzle of cream.

  5. Serve: Serve hot with naan, paratha, or rice.
